Seoul ADR Festival

The Seoul ADR Festival (SAF) was first launched in 2015 under the support of the Korean Commercial Arbitration Board (KCAB) and since then it has grown into an internationally recognized ADR event. Composed of various sessions addressing the process of dispute resolution from diverse perspectives, the SAF provides a platform for legal professionals, academics, and users to gather and exchange ideas on how to better accommodate the growing demand for ADR services and develop dispute resolution practices around the world.
