  1. Women's Interest Committee

  • Mission

    KCAB Women's Interest Committee was established to provide a ongoing forum to discuss issues that pertain not only to women arbitrators and practitioners, but everyone interested in the future of Korean arbitration community.

    Topics such as career development and training, enhancing the practice and advocacy standards and attaining higher diversity in the pool of participants in the field of international arbitration are just some examples. The Women's Interest Committee will also serve as a contact point for liasioning with groups in other jurisdictions around the world with similar interests.

  • Activities and Projects

    KCAB Women's Interest Committee is planning to hold seminars, training workshops and mentorshp programs throughout the year. Activities by the committee is open to everyone regardless of gender, age, profession or nationality.

    Those with interest in career development, industry best practices and diversity are encouraged to become involved and to participate in the discussions.
