International Arbitration Court

The International Advisory Council (the “Council”) is an advisory body of KCAB International, composed of leading experts in international dispute resolution. Its members provide high level guidance on KCAB International’s long term development, future initiatives, dispute resolution procedures, and the enhancement of its services and practices.
