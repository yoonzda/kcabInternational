  1. Fees

    Under Article 54 of the KCAB International Arbitration Rules, Parties are requested to pay the estimated arbitration costs in advance, in the manner determined by the Secretary-General.

  2. Fees

    Under Article 51 of the KCAB International Arbitration Rules, Parties are requested to pay the estimated arbitration costs in advance, in the manner and within the period of time determined by the Secretariat. The deposits constitute the initiation of the arbitral proceedings. The remainder of the costs is refunded when the case is closed.
