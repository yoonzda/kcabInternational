  1. Application to the Mediator Panel

    Download Mediation Request Form (English)

    The parties may jointly nominate a mediator from or outside KCAB’s Panel of Mediators to conduct the mediation The nomination shall be confirmed by KCAB. The parties may nominate or request the appointment of more than one mediator. The mediator’s main role in mediation is to facilitate communication between the parties to resolve the existing dispute.
